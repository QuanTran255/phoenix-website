Our Platform

DolphyAI is a comprehensive safety intelligence system designed specifically for the construction industry.

Intelligent Vision System

Powered by state-of-the-art object detection model with DINOv2 backbone, DolphyAI achieves superior small-object detection critical for construction safety. The system processes 4K video in real-time using advanced slicing technology, maintaining perfect clarity while running efficient edge inference on NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano, delivering real-time results directly on-site.

Safety Rules Engine

Translates raw visual data into actionable safety intelligence. Our geometric rules engine detects proximity hazards, PPE violations, unsafe positioning, and environmental risks. Every alert is automatically classified by severity and matched to OSHA standards with full evidence documentation.

Cloud Intelligence Platform

Centralized storage and analysis of all safety events. Your data is securely ingested via API, archived in our PostgreSQL system, and supplemented with AI-powered insights. Query your safety data naturally—our LLM agent translates plain language into instant reports and trend analysis.

Real-Time Dashboard

Beautiful React-based interface providing instant visibility into site safety. Monitor live alerts, review historical incidents, analyze trends, and generate compliance reports—all in one unified platform accessible from any device.

Synthetic Training & Robustness

Leveraging NVIDIA Omniverse, DolphyAI is trained on photorealistic synthetic construction scenarios representing diverse environments, lighting conditions, and hazardous situations. This ensures exceptional accuracy and robustness across all real-world site conditions without relying on dangerous incident recording.
